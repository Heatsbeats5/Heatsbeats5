Grade School

The School I’ve enrolled is Ormoc City Central School (OCCS) since the school is near our Barangay where we live and our barangay is just at the back of the school. In my elementary days I am one of the noisiest or talkative among my classmate. These days I experience super typhoon Yolanda we evacuated in the school during the typhoon. After the typhoon pass, we sleep at the hotel where our aunt is staying so we are comfortable to sleep and, in the morning, I’ve seen the devastation the typhoon has bring luckily theirs no one in the family got hurt. Also, in these days we welcome our new youngest member of our family my sister princess. After that my mother has a disease that needs operation so every day after school, we visited her in the hospital where she has been admitted.

Junior High School

In the Junior High School days, I enrolled on Western Leyte College (WLC). I enrolled there because my parents graduated there so they have trust that the school well provide good education for me but these days since I am a teenager sometimes, I cut class so we can play games in the computer shop. There was even a time that we are caught by our teacher’s and principal. But in these days one of the most tragic moments, I couldn’t forget is the day when my father dies this day, I feel like I am not myself anymore that something is missing from me. But I got through it somehow but from that day I became a silent type of person and I don’t like to go outside anymore. I couldn’t forget that day but we need to go forward because I know my father would want me to be successful someday. In these days also I am an honor student not always though because sometimes the lesson is harder.

Senior High School

In Senior High School, I enrolled on Ormoc City Senior High School (OCSHS) because of the financial problem cause by when my mother loss her job so I transferred in the public school to stabilize our financial needs. In these days I meet new classmates and meet new friends. I also learned many skills during these days related to computers. I graduated honor student but since there is no graduation cause by the pandemic. There is no graduation ceremony we just take the certificate then that’s all we didn’t even see our classmate one last time.
